About SakhiCircle 🌸

Welcome to SakhiCircle

SakhiCircle is a student-developed initiative that uses technology to promote menstrual hygiene awareness in a fun and interactive way. Built by engineering students, this project showcases how innovation can drive social good.

Our Mission 💖

“To break taboos, educate with empathy, and empower through awareness.”

We aim to make menstrual health education approachable, inclusive, and tech-enabled — empowering young minds to learn, share, and support one another.
